Algebra & Functions

Linear Equations:
- y = mx + b
- m = slope
- b = y-intercept

Quadratic Equations:
- ax^2 + bx + c = 0
- Quadratic Formula:
  x = (-b ± sqrt(b^2 - 4ac)) / (2a)

Derivatives:
- Rate of change
- Power Rule:
  d/dx (x^n) = n*x^(n-1)

Integrals:
- Area under the curve

Limits:
- Describes behavior as x approaches a value

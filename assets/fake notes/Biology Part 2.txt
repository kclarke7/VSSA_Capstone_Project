Biology Part 2

Cell Membrane: Controls what enters and leaves the cell
Cytoplasm: Jelly-like fluid inside the cell
Chloroplast: Organelle where photosynthesis happens
Golgi Apparatus: Packages and transports proteins
Homeostasis: Maintaining a stable internal environment
Diffusion: Movement from high concentration to low concentration
Osmosis: Movement of water across a membrane
ATP: Main energy currency of the cell
